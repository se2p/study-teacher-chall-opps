Page 01

Welcome!

The aim of the study is to find out what challenges and opportunities primary school teachers see or have already experienced when introducing programming in primary school. These findings will help to decrease or master challenges and to enable or improve the opportunities and also help to adapt the curriculum of primary school students. The results of the study will be published at a conference.

Your profile to take part in the survey: Primary/Elementary school teacher who has already taught programming/coding in primary/elementary school.

No personal questions are asked and the remaining data is anonymised. All questions must be answered in order to complete the study.

There are 18 questions in total (mix of multiple choice and open questions), which take about 15 minutes to complete. There is an option to cancel at any time in the questionnaire, in which case the complete evaluation is lost.

Note: By programming we also mean coding.

Attention for Prolific-User: Please copy and paste the completion code at the end of the questionnaire into Prolific.

Thank you!
